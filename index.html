Welcome to The Modern Seaside Restaurant

Where Coastal Flavors Meet Unforgettable Moments—Serving You Fresh, Flavorful Delights for Over 18 Years!

Our Menu Book a Table

Savor the Taste, Embrace the View: Discover Coastal Dining at Its Finest

Nestled on Miami’s shores, The Modern Seaside Restaurant offers fresh, locally-sourced seafood, premium meats, and vegetarian dishes—all with stunning ocean views.

Enjoy a blend of classic and modern flavors, perfectly paired with wines, cocktails, or craft beers in a serene seaside setting.

Whether it’s a romantic date or family gathering, our friendly team ensures every moment is unforgettable while supporting sustainable local practices.

Join us for great food, breathtaking views, and lasting memories.

Why Us

Why Choose The Modern Seaside Restaurant?

Experience Coastal Dining Like Never Before—Where Every Meal Is a Celebration of Flavor and View!

01

Unmatched Coastal Dining Experience

Indulge in a unique blend of breathtaking ocean views and delectable cuisine. Our restaurant is perfectly situated to offer panoramic vistas of the shore, creating an unforgettable dining atmosphere. Whether you’re enjoying a casual lunch or a romantic dinner, the serene coastal backdrop enhances every meal.

02

Fresh and Local Ingredients

We pride ourselves on using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients. Our menu features a variety of seasonal seafood, premium meats, and fresh vegetables, all expertly prepared to bring out the best in every dish. Supporting local farmers and fishermen not only ensures top quality but also helps sustain the environment.

03

Exceptional Service and Ambience

At our restaurant, every guest is treated like family. Our dedicated staff is committed to providing impeccable service, ensuring that your dining experience is as delightful and stress-free as possible. Combined with our stylish and comfortable setting, we create the perfect environment for any sort of occasion.

Events

Host Your Own Events or Join Ours at The Modern Seaside Restaurant

From Private Celebrations to Community Gatherings, Experience Memorable Moments with Us!

Custom Parties

$222

Whether you're planning an unforgettable birthday bash, a sophisticated corporate gathering, or a romantic night under the stars, we’ve got you covered. Our beachside venue offers the perfect backdrop for any occasion, customized to your unique style and preferences. From intimate dinners to large-scale celebrations, our team will work with you to create an event that’s as special as the moments you’re celebrating. Let us take care of the details so you can focus on making memories.

Beach Barbecue Nights

$45 per person

Join us for our weekly Beach Barbecue Nights, where the ocean breeze meets the smoky aroma of grilled seafood and meats. Enjoy a relaxed, laid-back evening on the beach with tropical cocktails in hand, as live music or a DJ sets the perfect mood for a fun and casual night.

Sunset Dinner Cruise

$120 per person

Experience the magic of the coast at sunset with our exclusive Sunset Dinner Cruise. In partnership with a local boat operator, we offer a unique dining experience where you can savor a gourmet meal while sailing along the coastline. Watch the sun dip below the horizon, casting a golden glow over the water, as you indulge in a memorable evening.

Tropical Cocktail Making Class

$35 per person

Shake things up with our Tropical Cocktail Making Class! Learn the art of crafting classic tropical drinks like piña coladas, mojitos, and margaritas in a fun and interactive session. Our expert mixologists will guide you through the process, ensuring you leave with the skills to impress at your next party.

Beach Yoga & Brunch

$50 per person

Start your day with a rejuvenating Beach Yoga session followed by a delicious and healthy brunch. This event is perfect for health-conscious guests looking to connect with nature while practicing mindfulness. Enjoy the serene sound of the waves as you flow through your yoga practice, then refuel with a nutritious meal.

Bonfire & S'mores Night

$30 per person

Cozy up by the fire at our Bonfire & S'mores Night on the beach. Create your own s’mores while enjoying live acoustic music in a relaxed, intimate setting. This event is perfect for couples, families, and friends looking to unwind under the stars with the warmth of a crackling fire.

Live Music & Dance Nights

$20 entry fee

Dance the night away at our Live Music & Dance Nights! Enjoy the best beach-themed music, from reggae to surf rock, performed by talented local bands and musicians. With a dedicated dance area, you can let loose and enjoy the rhythms of the beach in a lively and energetic atmosphere.

Tiki Night

$50 per person

Escape to a tropical paradise with our Tiki Night! Our restaurant will be transformed with themed decorations, offering a menu of exotic drinks and Polynesian-inspired dishes. Dress in your best tiki attire and compete for prizes, while enjoying the festive atmosphere of an island getaway.

Beach Movie Nights

$10 per person

Enjoy a classic movie under the stars at our Beach Movie Nights. We’ll set up a projector and screen to showcase beach-themed films that everyone loves. Grab a blanket, some popcorn, and cozy up on the sand for a nostalgic and relaxing evening by the ocean.

Meet Our Master Chefs

Discover the talented culinary artists behind our exceptional dishes.

Chef Marco

Chef Marco is an expert in crafting coastal cuisine, bringing the freshest flavors of the sea to every plate. His dishes are a perfect blend of tradition and innovation, capturing the essence of beachside dining with every bite.

Chef Marcus Samuelsson

Chef Marcus Samuelsson brings a vibrant fusion of global flavors to our beachside menu. With a deep passion for fresh, locally sourced seafood, he crafts dishes that are as visually appealing as they are flavorful, perfectly capturing the spirit of coastal dining.

Chef Luis

Chef Luis is a culinary artist known for his innovative approach to coastal cuisine. With a passion for fresh seafood and tropical flavors, he creates dishes that are not only delectable but also beautifully plated, bringing the essence of the beach to every meal.

Chef Nick Liberato

Chef Nick Liberato is renowned for his mastery of coastal cuisine, blending bold flavors with fresh, local ingredients. His passion for creating vibrant, mouthwatering dishes ensures that every plate reflects the spirit of the sea and sand, offering a memorable dining experience.

Chef Kristen Kish

Chef Kristen Kish brings a unique touch to our culinary team with her innovative approach to coastal cuisine. She excels in crafting dishes that balance intricate flavors and textures, delivering an unforgettable dining experience inspired by the freshness of the sea.

Chef Maria

Chef Maria infuses her passion for fresh, local ingredients into every dish. Known for her mastery of seafood, she creates vibrant and flavorful dishes that capture the essence of the ocean, making each meal a true work of art.
